It All Started When I Was Born...

Before moving to Nebraska I spent a brief stint in Denver as a dean's assistant at the Colorado School of Traditional Chinese Medicine (I didn't see it coming either), and before that I was an undergraduate at Willamette University in Salem, Oregon. Before that I was one of the few fifth graders at Highland Park Elementary to obtain a perfect score on his Greek Mythology exam, but we don't need to go back that far.

Here in Lincoln I spend most of my time outside of UNL jumping from one hyperfixation to the next, the fruits of which are scattered throughout this site. I also climb at the local gym, volunteer at the high school, and teach algebra at the local penitentiary. I make up for this by refusing to eat or buy local under any circumstances. Life is all about balance.
