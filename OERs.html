Currently in Development

Modern Algebra

  • Group Theory: Groups, Homomorphisms, Normal and Quotient Groups, Group Actions, Sylow Theory, Direct and Semidirect Products
  • Ring Theory: Rings, Homomorphisms, Quotients, Prime and Maximal Ideals, Polynomial Rings, Domains, Irreducibility
  • Module Theory: Modules, Homomorphisms, Bases, Vector Spaces, Linear Transformations, Classifications over PIDs, Canonical Forms
  • Field Theory: Fields, Field Extensions, Algebraic Closures, Splitting Fields, Separability, Galois Theory

Postmodern Algebra

  • Commutative Algebra: Finiteness Conditions, Graded Rings, Prime Spectra, Affine Varieties, Localization, Primary Decomposition, Dimension Theory
  • Algebraic Homology: Categories, Short Exact Sequences, Tensors, Projecive, Injective, Flat Modules, Resolutions, Abelian Categories, Ext and Tor
  • Symbolic Powers: Primary Decomposition, Symbolic Powers, Monomial Ideals, Rees Algebras, Ideal Powers, Prime Characteristic, The Containment Problem
  • Differential Operators: Derivations, Kahler Differentials, Regular Rings, Coefficient Fields, Complete Local Rings

Algebraic Number Theory: Topics in development

Linear Algebra: Matrices, Vector Spaces, Bases, Dimension and Rank-Nullity, Linear Transformations, Eigenvectors and Eigenvalues, Orthogonality, Inner Product Spaces

Foundations and Axiomatic Set Theory

  • Foundations of Advanced Mathematics: Formal Logic, Proofs, Sets, Functions, Relations, Induction and Recursion, Cardinality, Binary Operations
  • Axiomatic Set Theory: The Langauge of Sets, ZFC Axioms, Natural Numbers, Ordinals Numbers, Elementary Submodels, Constructability
